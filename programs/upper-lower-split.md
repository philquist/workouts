# Upper/Lower Split (4x/week)

Baseline training program — strength + hypertrophy focus.

## Schedule
- **Monday**: Upper A
- **Wednesday**: Lower A
- **Friday**: Upper B
- **Sunday**: Lower B

## Upper A
1. Bench Press: 4×6-8
2. Barbell Row: 4×8-10
3. Overhead Press: 3×8-10
4. Pull-ups: 3×AMRAP
5. Tricep Pushdowns: 3×12-15
6. Bicep Curls: 3×12-15

## Lower A
1. Squat: 4×6-8
2. Romanian Deadlift: 3×8-10
3. Leg Press: 3×10-12
4. Leg Curl: 3×12-15
5. Standing Calf Raise: 4×15-20

## Upper B
1. Incline Dumbbell Press: 4×8-10
2. Lat Pulldown: 4×10-12
3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3×10-12
4. Cable Row: 3×12-15
5. Lateral Raises: 3×15-20
6. Face Pulls: 3×15-20

## Lower B
1. Deadlift: 4×5
2. Front Squat or Leg Press: 3×10-12
3. Walking Lunges: 3×12 each leg
4. Leg Extension: 3×12-15
5. Seated Calf Raise: 4×15-20

## Notes
- Rest 2-3 min between compound sets, 60-90s for accessories
- Progressive overload: add weight when you hit top of rep range for all sets
- Adjust volume/intensity based on recovery
